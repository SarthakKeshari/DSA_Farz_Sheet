Ultimate DSA Sheet + Tracker

💡 Welcome! How to use this tracker (Click to collapse/expand)
  • Track Progress: Use the "Status" dropdown to mark problems as Not Done, Review, or Done. Add custom notes in the "Comments" column. Your progress saves automatically to your browser!
  • Search & Filter: Find specific problems fast using the search bar, or filter by Topic and Difficulty.
  • 🎲 Random Question: Click this to get a random problem. The topic is hidden so you can practice identifying the algorithmic pattern from scratch.
  • 🤖 AI Practice: Click "Gen Prompt" to copy a detailed prompt. Paste it into your preferred local on-device model or web chatbot to test your understanding with a uniquely generated practice question.
  • 💾 Backup & Restore: Use Export/Import Progress to save your data as a file so you don't lose it if you clear your browser history or switch machines.
